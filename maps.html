MAPS

The Skeld is the primary and first map created in Among Us, set on a spaceship in space.

MIRA HQ is the second map of Among Us, set high in the Earth's atmosphere. The facility is owned by the recurring company MIRA.

Polus is the third map in Among Us, which is set on the planet Polus as a planetary base.
